A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

I’m not very good at retaining information that I have read or that has been demonstrated to me. I need to do things myself to learn. I’m pretty good at retaining skills and adapting them to fit new purposes once learned. I can get really stuck into things to the point that I forget to give myself breaks. Time management is important!

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Teamwork and in particular communicating regularly. I work well independently but need to remember to check in with my team/facilitators often.

How will you will manage your workload in this programme?

I need to make sure I am managing life outside of course work effectively so I have sufficient time to complete tasks and be available for communications with others involved in the learning process. I also need to remember to take regular breaks. When things are building up I will make sure to be transparent with facilitators and team members. When I need to seek help I can do so in discord and either post about the issue or chat with people in the help desk or common spaces. I’ll make sure to do this if I notice myself getting stuck for 20 minutes or more.
